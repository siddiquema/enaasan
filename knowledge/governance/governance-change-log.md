# Governance Change Log

**Record of all changes to Enaasan governance policies.** This log is append-only; entries are never deleted or modified (only corrections are added as new entries).

Use semantic versioning: MAJOR.MINOR.PATCH.
- MAJOR: new governance document (e.g., GOV-002)
- MINOR: significant change to existing policy (e.g., GOV-001 adds new requirement)
- PATCH: clarification or correction (e.g., GOV-001 typo fix)

---

## v1.0 (2026-07-14)

**Document:** GOV-001: Research and Intellectual Property Policy  
**Author:** Founder / Chief Knowledge Architect  
**Status:** Approved  
**Rationale:** Enaasan's reputation depends on trust and originality. IP compliance and research rigor are non-negotiable. This policy formalizes the directives from 2026-07-14 into operational governance.

**Key provisions:**
- Every load-bearing claim verified against original sources
- Unverifiable claims are removed, not weakened
- Original voice required; quotations minimal and attributed
- All media assets licensed; licensing status clear or asset excluded
- Write every artifact to publishable/legally-defensible standard
