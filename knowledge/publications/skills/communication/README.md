# Communication Skills Knowledge Cards

A series of five interconnected Knowledge Cards about why communication competency matters in the Indian job market — and how to assess and develop it.

---

## The Five Cards

### **1. Definition: What is Communication Competency?**
📄 [01-what-is-communication-competency.md](01-what-is-communication-competency.md)

Defines communication competency as "the ability to exchange information, ideas, and feedback effectively with others" and breaks it into 4 dimensions: Clarity, Empathy, Engagement, Consistency.

**Outcome:** Reader understands communication is more than speaking English well.

---

### **2. Framework: The 5 Communication Competencies Employers Value**
📄 [02-five-communication-competencies.md](02-five-communication-competencies.md)

Explains the 5 specific competencies employers measure:
1. Active Listening
2. Clarity & Conciseness  
3. Emotional Intelligence / Empathy
4. Persuasion & Influence
5. Adaptability in Communication

Includes self-assessment tool to rate yourself 1-5 on each.

**Outcome:** Reader can self-assess strengths and gaps, and identify what to develop.

---

### **3. Mental Model: Why Communication Skills Command a 20–30% Salary Premium**
📄 [03-why-communication-commands-premium.md](03-why-communication-commands-premium.md)

Explains the economic mechanism: Communication competency reduces business costs (rework, escalations, misunderstandings) and increases revenue (retention, efficiency). Shows salary premiums by role level.

**Outcome:** Reader understands communication skills aren't soft/optional; they have measurable, quantifiable business value.

---

### **4. Checklist: Am I Ready for a Communication-Heavy Role?**
📄 [04-am-i-ready-checklist.md](04-am-i-ready-checklist.md)

50-item self-assessment checklist across the 5 competencies. Scoring helps you assess readiness for customer-facing, leadership, or sales roles. Includes development plan if gaps exist.

**Outcome:** Reader knows if they're ready for communication-heavy roles now, or what to work on first.

---

### **5. Example: How Salary Ranges Shift with Communication Quality in BPS**
📄 [05-salary-impact-bps-example.md](05-salary-impact-bps-example.md)

Three real-world examples showing salary impact:
- Example 1: Customer Service Rep (₹23.5k vs ₹29.5k/month)
- Example 2: Finance Analyst (₹41k vs ₹46k/month, different career trajectory)
- Example 3: Team Lead (₹57.5k vs ₹66k/month, 15% difference)

Plus 20-year career earnings comparison: ₹1.3-2 Crore difference over a career.

**Outcome:** Reader sees concrete rupee amounts and career impact from communication competency.

## How These Cards Work Together

**Sequence for reading:**
1. Start with Card 1 (Definition) — understand what we're talking about
2. Read Card 2 (Framework) — see what specifically to assess
3. Read Card 4 (Checklist) — self-assess against the framework
4. Read Card 3 (Mental Model) — understand WHY this matters (motivation to develop)
5. Read Card 5 (Example) — see concrete salary/career impact

**Reusability:**
- Card 1 + Card 2 can appear in "Career Foundations" learning paths
- Card 2 can appear in "Self-Assessment" or "Pre-Job-Search" paths
- Card 4 (Checklist) can appear in "Ready for BPS" or "Customer-Facing Roles" paths
- Card 5 (Example) can appear in "BPS Career Guides" or "Salary Negotiation" paths
- Card 3 (Mental Model) can appear in "Skills Worth Developing" or "Career ROI" paths
