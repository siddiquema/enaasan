# publications/careers/

## Purpose

Real publications covering career exploration generally — Career Profiles, Career Roadmaps, and industry/field-level content not tied to one specific life stage. Corresponds to the `CAREER` audience code in `architecture/document-id-standard.md`, and to the `/careers` topic hub concept in `docs/website-update-spec.md` §10.

## Contents

Empty today. Each future publication gets its own subfolder, named by slug.

## Naming Convention

`publications/careers/<slug>/source.md`.

## Rules

Same as the parent `publications/README.md`. Career Profiles specifically follow `templates/career-profile-template.md` and must include the salary-disclaimer language it specifies.

## Examples

- The existing BPS/KPO career cards on `careers.html` (Customer Service Representative, Back Office/Data Processing Executive, Finance & Accounts Analyst, Research Analyst, HR Operations Executive) are the natural first candidates for formal Career Profiles here, each becoming its own `publications/careers/<role-slug>/source.md`.

## Future Expansion

- Once several Career Profiles exist, this folder is the natural home for Career Roadmaps connecting them (e.g. a roadmap spanning Customer Service Representative → Team Lead → Operations Manager), and for the Comparison Pages described in `docs/website-update-spec.md` §12 once that content type has a template.
