# publications/workplace/

## Purpose

Real publications covering early-career and workplace-readiness content — the skills, expectations, and adjustments involved in actually starting and succeeding in a first job, as distinct from `graduates/`'s focus on the job *search* itself. Corresponds to the `WORK` audience code in `architecture/document-id-standard.md`.

## Contents

Empty today. Each future publication gets its own subfolder, named by slug.

## Naming Convention

`publications/workplace/<slug>/source.md`.

## Rules

Same as the parent `publications/README.md`.

## Examples

- A future Guide such as "What to expect in your first three months at work" or a Checklist for "before your first day," extending naturally from where the existing 90-Day Career Clarity Plan's "After day 90" section leaves off.

## Future Expansion

- Likely early content: Knowledge Cards for workplace terms (probation, appraisal cycle, notice period), a Playbook for common early-career friction scenarios (disagreeing with a manager, asking for feedback, handling a slow start).
