# Playbook Template

For a scenario/decision-driven document — "if you're in situation X, here's how to think about it and what to do," often covering several branching scenarios under one theme (distinct from a Guide, which explains a topic more linearly, and from a Workbook, which assumes a fixed sequence of stages everyone follows).

Copy the block from `metadata-template.md` above this line, with `document_id` using type `PLAYBOOK`.

---

# [Playbook Title]

[One or two sentences: what range of situations this playbook helps with, and for whom.]

## How this playbook works

[Explain that the reader should find the scenario closest to their own rather than reading start to finish, if that's the intended usage — Playbooks are often reference-like in this way, similar to a Reference Guide but organised by scenario rather than by topic.]

## Scenario: [Name the situation plainly, as the reader would describe it]

**If this is you:** [One or two sentences describing who this scenario applies to.]

**What to think about:** [The reasoning/considerations relevant to this scenario.]

**What to do:** [Concrete next step(s).]

> **Honest note:** [Any caveat specific to this scenario.]

## Scenario: [Repeat the pattern for each distinct scenario]

[...]
