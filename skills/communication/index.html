Key Insight

Communication competency is not soft or optional. It has measurable, quantifiable business value. Organizations lose projects and money to poor communication. Communication skills command a premium because they're hard to fake, hard to develop quickly, in high demand but short supply, and scale with seniority.

Start with the definition guide. Then take the self-assessment. Then focus your practice on your weakest two competencies.
